About Dr. Filip Čaniga

Board-certified plastic surgeon
with attention to detail

I am a board-certified plastic surgeon practising in Bratislava. I cover the full spectrum of aesthetic and reconstructive surgery — from breast augmentation and liposuction to blepharoplasty, otoplasty, and more complex procedures such as abdominoplasty.

I take an individual approach with every patient — I believe the best outcomes come from collaboration and open conversation. For breast augmentation I use the modern Preservé™ method, which prioritises gentleness and a natural result.

Quality is essential to me, so I keep learning continuously — attending international congresses and masterclass workshops, including in Marbella and Düsseldorf. I speak fluent English and German.
